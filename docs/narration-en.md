# TraceMind · English Voice-Over Script (v1.0)

Companion to `tracemind-video/` (the Remotion project). Twelve scenes, 2160 frames @ 30fps, ~140 spoken words.

## Clean Script

> In financial research, the hard part isn't writing the report.
> It's making every line stand up to scrutiny.
>
> TraceMind.
>
> It turns investment research into an auditable chain —
> every thought, every tool call, every conclusion bound to a source.
>
> You see the main thesis. You also see the alternative.
> You see where the audit rules stop the model from overreaching.
> Every citation is checked against the source it came from.
>
> Risk is layered. The decision card delivers a clear rating,
> a confidence, and indicators to track.
>
> The knowledge graph connects companies, customers, and regulators
> into one navigable network.
>
> Follow-up questions build on prior evidence — no starting from scratch.
>
> Research is no longer a black box.
> It's a path you can walk back.
>
> TraceMind. Every judgment, traceable.

## Voice Direction

| Field | Setting |
|---|---|
| Tempo | 140 wpm baseline; slow to 125 wpm on S01, S02, S12 |
| Tone | calm, observational, never enthusiastic |
| Pitch | low-mid; male 100–135 Hz / female 165–195 Hz |
| Sentence shape | declarative; falling cadence at full stops; quick breath on em-dash |
| Emphasis | *report* · *every line* · *auditable chain* · *bound to a source* · *also see the alternative* · *stop the model* · *walked back* · *traceable* |
| Mic | close-medium dynamic; final LUFS -16, VO sits +9 dB above music bed |

## Pronunciation Notes

- **navigable** — `/ˈnæv.ɪ.ɡə.bəl/`
